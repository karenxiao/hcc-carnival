Directions

Public transportation is highly recommended, as the SOCH is approximately a 10-15 minute walk from the nearest T station.

From the Red Line: Disembark at Harvard Square station, following signs to the Church St exit. Follow Massachusetts Avenue towards the fork of Massachusetts Ave and Garden St, and bear left to follow Garden St. Continue on Garden St, past the Sheraton Commander, until you see a sign for the "Quadrangle" on your right.  Take a right onto the residential street there, and our building (called Hilles, or the SOCH) is on your left.  We'll have signs directing you to the main entrance, and the event will be on the top floor "Penthouse Level".

 

Parking

Parking in Harvard Square can be found in parking garages or parking lots. There is a parking garage on the corner of JFK St and Eliot St, and a parking lot on Church St.

There is also a parking lot behind the SOCH building located at the elementary school, where you may be able to find free parking.
